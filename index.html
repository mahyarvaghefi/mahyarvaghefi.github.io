Mahyar Sharif Vaghefi

Assistant Professor of Information Systems

Department of Information Systems & Operations Management
College of Business · University of Texas at Arlington

Information Systems Machine Learning Data Science Online Social Networks Marketing Analytics Crowd Sourcing

My research explores the intersection of information systems, machine learning, and online social networks. I study how individuals interact in digital environments — from self-disclosure and impression management on social platforms to health information dissemination and crowdsourcing dynamics. I develop computational methods including node embedding techniques for social network analysis and decision support frameworks.
