Welcome

Before diving into the content, take a few minutes to watch the introduction above. It sets the stage for what you’ll learn and why it matters — even if you’re skeptical about AI right now.

Why This Course?

AI is already reshaping how students learn, how educators teach, and how institutions operate. This course is designed to give you a practical, honest foundation — not hype, not fear — so you can make informed decisions about AI in your own teaching practice.

What You’ll Learn in This Course

The course is organized into four chapters, each building on the last. Together they take you from understanding what AI actually is to developing your own policies and practices around it.

01

Why AI Literacy Matters NOW

The reality of AI in education, cognitive traps, terminology, limitations, and privacy considerations.

02

Prompt Engineering Fundamentals

How to communicate with AI effectively to get useful results.

03

Integrating AI Effectively in Your Teaching

Practical strategies for incorporating AI into your courses and assignments.

04

Ethics & Policy

Guiding students on ethical AI use and developing clear policies.

How to Use This Course

Each chapter includes a video lecture and a companion guide like this one. Watch the video first, then use the guide to review key concepts, reference tables, and practical examples at your own pace.

Key Takeaways

✓AI is already here — students are using it whether we address it or not.
✓Literacy over fear: understanding AI’s capabilities and limitations empowers better teaching decisions.
✓Four chapters take you from foundations to practical classroom integration and policy development.
✓No technical background needed — this course is designed for educators across all disciplines.
✓Companion guides pair with each video so you can review concepts at your own pace.
